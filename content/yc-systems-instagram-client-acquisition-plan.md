# YC Systems Instagram Client Acquisition Plan

## Goal

Instagram should attract business owners who want to improve operations, automate work, launch a website, build an online store, create an internal system, or develop a SaaS/app.

The feed should not feel like a project gallery. Projects and products are proof, but the message is:

YC Systems builds digital solutions for businesses that want to operate better, sell better and grow with more control.

## Profile Highlights

Use these highlights in this order:

1. Inicio
2. Webs
3. Sistemas
4. Auto
5. Tiendas
6. SaaS
7. Soporte
8. Contacto

Assets:

- Covers: `assets/social/client-acquisition-highlights/*-cover.jpg`
- Stories: `assets/social/client-acquisition-highlights/*-story.jpg`

## Feed Direction

Primary themes:

- Business problem
- Clear solution
- Expected result
- Simple CTA

Examples:

- "Tu negocio necesita una web que venda confianza."
- "Si todo depende de Excel y WhatsApp, necesitas un sistema."
- "Automatizar no es lujo, es orden."
- "Una tienda online debe vender, no solo verse bonita."
- "Un SaaS empieza con un proceso real."
- "El mantenimiento evita que tu presencia digital se quede atras."

## Proof Strategy

Products and projects are used as proof:

- CleanLoop proves laundry operations can be systemized.
- SOC proves dashboards and commercial operations can be centralized.
- BrokerControl proves CRM and follow-up can be structured.
- GhostWear proves a client brand can become an online store.

But the CTA should speak to the client:

- "Quiero una web."
- "Quiero automatizar."
- "Quiero un sistema interno."
- "Quiero una tienda online."
- "Quiero un SaaS MVP."
- "Quiero mantenimiento."

## Posting Rhythm

Two posts daily:

- Morning: business pain or education.
- Night: solution, offer or CTA.

Keep captions Spanish-first, clear, professional and direct.
