# YC Systems Instagram Identity System

## Objective

Make YC Systems look like a premium software product company, not a scattered portfolio.

## New Direction: Nexus Studio

The primary feed identity is now **Nexus Studio**.

Goal:

- Look like a premium SaaS/product company.
- Make Nexus the visual guide without making the brand childish.
- Show products as an ecosystem, not as random screenshots.
- Use fewer posts with stronger sales intent.

Style:

- Dark editorial tech poster.
- Large readable headline.
- Nexus or product mockup as the main visual.
- Product cards or value chips at the bottom.
- Minimal copy inside the image.
- Strong contrast: white, YC blue, cyan and small green accents.

Primary assets:

- `assets/social/active-feed/01-web-confianza.jpg`
- `assets/social/active-feed/02-sistema-excel-whatsapp.jpg`
- `assets/social/active-feed/03-automatizar-orden.jpg`
- `assets/social/active-feed/04-tienda-vende.jpg`

These are the only assets the publishing queue should use for feed posts.

Archived assets live under:

- `assets/social/archive/`

Do not use archived assets for automation unless they are intentionally restored.

## Feed Structure

Use a clean repeating rhythm:

1. Brand / Nexus
2. Product
3. Product
4. Product
5. Case study
6. Business problem

Secondary educational posts may still follow:

1. Problema
2. Solucion
3. Resultado

But they must use the Nexus Studio look, not the older green-circle template.

- Sistemas internos
- CleanLoop
- BrokerControl
- SOC
- E-commerce / GhostWear
- Mantenimiento

## Visual Rules

- Dark navy background.
- YC electric blue.
- Cyan highlights.
- Green CTA only for action/result.
- Same typography hierarchy.
- Nexus appears as a guide, not as a childish mascot.
- One clear message per post.
- No isolated screenshots without context.

## Copy Rules

Every caption must include:

- Business pain.
- YC Systems solution.
- Expected result.
- One CTA.

Examples:

- Escribe SISTEMA.
- Escribe CLEANLOOP.
- Escribe CRM.
- Escribe DASHBOARD.
- Iniciar proyecto.

## Nexus Role

Nexus is the visual assistant of YC Systems.

Message:

Nexus convierte operaciones reales en productos digitales claros, utiles y listos para crecer.

Use Nexus in:

- Educational posts.
- Stories.
- Highlight covers.
- Technology explanations.
- Onboarding.
- Presentations.

Do not overuse Nexus in every post. It should reinforce the brand, not replace the products.
