# YC Systems Instagram Identity System

## Objective

Make YC Systems look like a premium software product company, not a scattered portfolio.

## Feed Structure

Use a repeating grid:

1. Problema
2. Solucion
3. Resultado

Repeat by product or solution:

- Sistemas internos
- CleanLoop
- BrokerControl
- SOC
- E-commerce / GhostWear
- Mantenimiento

## Visual Rules

- Dark navy background.
- YC electric blue.
- Cyan highlights.
- Green CTA only for action/result.
- Same typography hierarchy.
- Nexus appears as a guide, not as a childish mascot.
- One clear message per post.
- No isolated screenshots without context.

## Copy Rules

Every caption must include:

- Business pain.
- YC Systems solution.
- Expected result.
- One CTA.

Examples:

- Escribe SISTEMA.
- Escribe CLEANLOOP.
- Escribe CRM.
- Escribe DASHBOARD.
- Iniciar proyecto.

## Nexus Role

Nexus is the visual assistant of YC Systems.

Message:

Nexus convierte operaciones reales en productos digitales claros, utiles y listos para crecer.

Use Nexus in:

- Educational posts.
- Stories.
- Highlight covers.
- Technology explanations.
- Onboarding.
- Presentations.

Do not overuse Nexus in every post. It should reinforce the brand, not replace the products.

## Highlight Order

1. Products
2. Cases
3. Clients
4. Technology
5. Contact
6. Start Here
