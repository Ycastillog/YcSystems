# YC Systems Sales Content Framework

## Goal

The content should create qualified conversations, not just fill the feed.

Every post must answer at least one of these questions:

- What business problem does this solve?
- Who is this product or solution for?
- What result can the client expect?
- What should the viewer do next?

## Content Funnel

### 1. Problem
Use when the audience needs to recognize pain.

Examples:
- Your business depends on Excel and WhatsApp.
- Your team loses client follow-up.
- Your website exists but does not generate trust.
- You cannot see your operation in one dashboard.

CTA:
- "Escribe SISTEMA"
- "Escribe CRM"
- "Escribe DASHBOARD"

### 2. Product
Use when showing YC Systems as a product company.

Products:
- CleanLoop: laundry operations platform.
- SOC: sales operations center.
- BrokerControl: real estate CRM.
- CreditPilot: credit repair platform in development.

CTA:
- "Conoce el producto"
- "Hablemos de un piloto"
- "Dime si tu operacion se parece a esta"

### 3. Proof
Use when showing credibility.

Proof assets:
- GhostWear as client ecommerce case.
- LucianoWash as service-business digital presence.
- Real screenshots and active domains.
- Founder and company vision.

CTA:
- "Ver caso"
- "Quiero algo como esto"

### 4. Offer
Use when asking for action directly.

Offers:
- Website ready to sell.
- Online store.
- Internal system.
- CRM/dashboard.
- SaaS MVP.
- Maintenance.

CTA:
- "Escribe PROYECTO"
- "Pide una propuesta inicial"

## Weekly Balance

- 30% product company authority.
- 30% business education.
- 25% product/case proof.
- 15% direct offer.

## Quality Rule

Do not publish generic content if it does not connect to:

- CleanLoop
- SOC
- BrokerControl
- GhostWear
- LucianoWash
- websites
- ecommerce
- CRM
- dashboards
- SaaS
- automation
- maintenance

## Replies To Use Manually

When someone comments "SISTEMA":
"Gracias. Cuentame que proceso quieres organizar: clientes, pagos, reservas, tareas, reportes o seguimiento?"

When someone comments "TIENDA":
"Perfecto. Que tipo de productos vendes y ya tienes fotos/catalogo o hay que prepararlo desde cero?"

When someone comments "CRM":
"Bien. Tu flujo es para ventas, inmobiliaria, servicios o seguimiento interno?"

When someone comments "DASHBOARD":
"Excelente. Que numeros necesitas ver cada dia para tomar mejores decisiones?"

When someone comments "PROYECTO":
"Claro. Cuentame en una frase que quieres construir y para que negocio seria."
